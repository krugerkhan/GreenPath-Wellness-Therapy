About Us

Get To Know Us

GreenPath Wellness was born out of a shared passion for helping people who have navigated the challenging journey of surviving narcissistic abuse. I too have walked this difficult path. I understand the profound impact that such experiences can have on one's well being, and it is this personal understanding that fuels our commitment to providing a sanctuary for healing, growth, and empowerment. GreenPath Wellness is not just a paltform but a supportive community where, we come together to share their stories, find solace in shared experiences, and embark on a journey towards healing. We invite survivors to explore the therapeutic benefits of connecting with nature, in a community of like-minded individuals who understand what we have been through.

We are not just a team of therapists and guidance counsellor we are a group of individuals who deeply care about making a positive impact. Our team includes licensed therapists, outdoor enthusiasts, and folks who believe in the transformative power of a walk in the woods.

Why Wilderness?

Nature has a unique way of speaking to the soul. It's in the rustle of leaves, the scent of pine, and the stillness of a mountain view. We've seen firsthand how the wilderness becomes a powerful ally in the journey of self-discovery. Incorporating the principles of wilderness therapy, we believe that nature holds an unparalleled capacity to facilitate healing.

GreenPath Activities

Your Journey, Your Way: No two journeys are the same. That's why we tailor our programs to your unique needs and goals. This is about you, and we are here to support you every step of the way.

Nature as Your Co-Therapist: The great outdoors isn't just a backdrop; it's an active participant in your healing journey. We've seen the profound impact of nature on personal growth, and we're excited to share it with you.
